If any of the following apply to you, it might be good to collaborate and work through these issues.

- You feel your career is going nowhere.
- You want to win.
- You want to live on your terms.
- You don't know what your terms are.
- You feel working in tech has no meaning.
- You want to find your purpose.
- You get too caught up in unimportant things.
- You feel left out by your colleagues.
- You don't feel taken seriously at work.
- You want to be successful by your own definition of success.
- You're scared.
- You have trouble dealing with DEI.
- You want to be a live player.
- You don't want to play politics.
- You do want to play politics.
- You want to increase your salary.

My coaching method will consist of tough love, frameworks drawn from foundational agile and buddhist works and gentle force. This is not meant to make you feel good.
