Like I [said yesterday](/dingen/2009/11/vergeet-niet-het-inchecken/), I added support for the Amsterdam subway to [fakesubwayapis](http://github.com/straup/gae-fakesubwayapis) and [fakesubwayapis-data](http://github.com/straup/gae-fakesubwayapis-data). My forks: [gae-fakesubwayapis](http://github.com/alper/gae-fakesubwayapis) and [gae-fakesubwayapis-data](http://github.com/alper/gae-fakesubwayapis-data).

I hope [Aaron](http://twitter.com/aaronofmontreal) includes this code sometime soon.

Further support including all the other transit modalities, GPS coordinates and routes should follow but no idea yet when I'll find the time.

Thanks [Kilian](http://kilianvalkhof.com/) for the indispensable [git-advice](http://twitter.com/alper/status/6235788906).

Tonight [Visible Cities](http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=188558907275) in De Verdieping, [Urban Screens](http://networkcultures.org/wpmu/urbanscreens/) this Friday at the same and [Terrains Vagues](http://upcoming.yahoo.com/event/4892816/UT/Utrecht/Terrains-Vagues-2/TUMULT/;_ylc=X3oDMTFtcTFyN3ZmBF9TAzk3MzQ4NTEwBF9wAzEEcGlkA0UtNDg5MjgxNgRwb3MDMwRzZWMDaG1lbXkEc2xrA2RldA--;_ylv=3) next week at Tumult in Utrecht.
