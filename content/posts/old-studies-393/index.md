I had a batch of final presentations for a course today, and the age old [adage by Tufte](http://web.archive.org/web/20040428012940/http://www.cs.washington.edu/homes/ajb/hci/tufte.html) still holds:

> Finish early!  Something good is bound to happen.

Now if only people would get a clue.
