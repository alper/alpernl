Two guys from capoeira have the same new Samsung phone. I saw them this week while they tried to figure out how to display a picture when you are called by somebody. They couldn't make it work.

The message to mobile phone makers is: If you neglect user friendliness long enough, Apple will come along and eat your lunch.
