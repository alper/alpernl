I forwarded this sheet from [Stephen Walli's presentation](http://stephesblog.blogs.com/presentations/GartnerOSSValue2.pdf) on the Gartner OSS Summit to a friend. We were having the discussion that selling people software based on cost reduction is bogus because in most cases that amount of cost reduction is never going to be realized and is not really that relevant.

Turns out we were right. From the deck:
