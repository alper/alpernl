Quickly this last week. Work on our own iPhone transit application is progressing nicely. The iPhone has an exquisite palette of operations to work with.

I met the great people from [Campus Party](http://www.campus-party.eu/) who are going to hold an event in Berlin at the end of August. More to follow in the light of hacking, programming and opening up the things that should be open.

[![Eric Zimmerman and Nathalie Pozzi presenting at the UdK](http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7092/7247847414_afd191a01f.jpg)](http://www.flickr.com/photos/alper/7247847414/ "Eric Zimmerman and Nathalie Pozzi presenting at the UdK by illustir, on Flickr")
