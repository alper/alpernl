It seems our mashup Last.TV is starting to lead a life of its own. After the final event, I spent half a night bringing it up to par functionally and feature wise so we have a tolerale offering right now, but some tweaking still remains to be done.

What are we going to do next? Some redesign work and stability on the site is still necessary. And a possible rebranding and domain name would be nice. The domain Last.TV has been registered by someone from [Last.FM](http://www.last.fm) and if they are not doing anything with it we would like to borrow it.
