Meer [goed advies van Guy](http://blog.guykawasaki.com/2006/01/hindsights_ii_t.html).

> Things are never as good or as bad as they seem.

Precies, geen paniek.

> If you think someone is an orifice, everyone else does too. & Life is too short to deal with orifices.

Filter je interacties en ga door met je leven.

> Judge others by their intentions and yourself by your results.

Deze had ik ook [via pinds](http://pinds.com/articles/2006/01/25/guy-kawasaki-judge-others-by-their-intentions-and-yourself-by-your-results) gelezen en dit komt tegelijkertijd zo vanzelfsprekend maar ook oh zo waardevol op me over.

> Never ask people to do something that you wouldn't do.

De gouden regel van het management.

**Rock on, Guy!**
