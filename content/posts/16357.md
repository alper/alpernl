Drastically lowering the voting age as Germany is considering here would solve a huge amount of problems (which is why it will of course not happen).

[David Runciman has argued](https://www.talkingpoliticspodcast.com/blog/2018/129-democracy-for-young-people) along similar lines that rejuvenating democracy usually happens by expanding the franchise, in this case to children.
