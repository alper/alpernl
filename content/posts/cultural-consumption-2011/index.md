I dived into my log to make the yearly tally of what I did and saw. All in all 2011 has proven to be a good year.

It was a bit of a slow movie year though. I only saw 56, the best of which were: ["Drive"](http://cineville.nl/films/drive), ["Melancholia"](http://cineville.nl/films/melancholia), ["Once Upon a Time in Anatolia"](http://cineville.nl/films/once-upon-time-anatolia), ["Blue Valentine"](http://cineville.nl/films/blue-valentine), ["Norwegian Wood"](http://cineville.nl/films/norwegian-wood), ["True Grit"](http://cineville.nl/films/true-grit), ["Almanya"](http://cineville.nl/films/almanya), ["Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy"](http://cineville.nl/films/tinker-tailor-soldier-spy) and ["Kosmos"](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1371574/).

I went to 32 plays in 2011. The best ones:

- ["Die Macht der Finsternis"](http://www.schaubuehne.de/de_DE/program/detail/9131246) in die Schaubühne
- ["Het meisje dat teveel van lucifers hield"](http://www.ntgent.be/productie/het-meisje-dat-te-veel-van-lucifers-hield) by NTGent in Frascati WG
- ["Songs at the End of the World"](http://www.wunderbaum.nl/Songs%20at%20the%20end%20of%20the%20world.html) by Wunderbaum in SSBA
- ["De Russen!"](http://www.toneelgroepamsterdam.nl/default.asp?path=cb3a2j7l) by Toneelgroep Amsterdam at the Holland Festival
- ["This is how you will disappear"](http://www.g-v.fr/creations/ve-thisishowyouwilldisappear-frameset.htm) by Gisèle Vienne in Stadsschouwburg Utrecht (back at the Kaai [in February](http://www.g-v.fr/ve-agenda.htm)!)
- ["Langs de grote weg"](http://www.barreland.nl/content/langs-de-grote-weg) by de Vere in Frascati

I read 21 books in 2011. The most notable of those were:

- ["The Life and Death of Great American Cities"](http://www.nytimes.com/books/97/08/17/reviews/jacobs.html?pagewanted=all) by Jane Jacobs
- ["The City & The City"](http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2009/may/30/china-mieville-fiction) by China Miville
- ["De kaart en het gebied"](http://www.vn.nl/boeken/fictie/de-kaart-en-het-gebied-michel-houellebecq/) by Michel Houellebecq
- ["Super Sad True Love Story"](http://supersadtruelovestory.com/) by Gary Shteyngart
- ["Cloud Atlas"](http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2004/mar/06/fiction.asbyatt) by David Mitchell
- ["The Sun Also Rises"](http://www.archive.org/details/sunalsorises030276mbp) by Ernest Hemingway
- ["Unit Operations"](http://www.bogost.com/books/unit_operations.shtml) by Ian Bogost

I started tracking the games I played around halfway through the year, so this is not an exhaustive list, but five games I really enjoyed last year were: ["Where is my Heart?"](http://gutefabrik.com/wimh.html), ["Nidhogg"](http://messhof.com/nidhogg/), ["Space Alert"](http://czechgames.com/en/space-alert/), ["The Binding of Isaac"](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Binding_of_Isaac_(video_game)) and ["The Resistance"](http://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/41114/the-resistance).
