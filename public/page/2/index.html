Iseji - Day 1 - Tamura to Misedani

The day started late after jetlag kept me up between 2-5am. Then when I finally got to sleep, it was time to get up and leave. The Fitbit clocks me at 3h47m of sleep.

I quickly walked over from the lovely Mizuhokan to the Ise Outer Shrine. The site is beautiful and I just learned that the empty lot next to the shrine is where they’ll build the building anew every 20 years. A tradition, shikinen sengu, I had heard about before but hadn’t connected to this place. They started the 63rd rebuilding this year apparently .
