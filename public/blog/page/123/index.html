I’ve changed my monthly contribution from the Guardian to the Taz. The Guardian is a bit weird and was not doing that much for me anymore. Taz, I only read occasionally but they’re local (with an office that is practically next to my house) and they’re a lot more left-wing than the Guardian is. I haven’t found many good alternative newspapers other than the FT which is ridiculously expensive.
