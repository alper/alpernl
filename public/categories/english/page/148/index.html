Culture flat rates are a horrible idea

Culture flat rates are named both in the Netherlands and in Germany as a solution to the problem of copyright piracy. The idea is to have everybody pay a set amount of money each month so that they can download all they want and redistribute that money among copyright holders to compensate them for their work. This stems from the notion that copyright holders deserve some payment for their work and that the current repression being employed to uphold the dysfunctional copyright system, is unacceptable. ...
