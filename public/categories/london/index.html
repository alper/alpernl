A government that's certain to win

I’m not that negative about the Dominic Cummings job ad, because: Unlike a lot of people in governments everywhere, he seems to have a clue what he is doing. That a person who’s winning would quote Boyd is not a coincidence. Using a blog in this way (or Trump tweeting like he does) is the direct e-government that everybody has always been asking for.
