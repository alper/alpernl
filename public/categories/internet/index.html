The show itself needs almost no introduction since it’s become a mainstream online phenomenon. Subway Takes features comedian and musician Kareem talking to NYC personalities who share their “takes” during brief subway rides.

He ventures outside of New York once in a while, and after London and Paris, has now turned up in Berlin.
